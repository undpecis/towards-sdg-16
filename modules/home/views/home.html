TOWARDS SDG 16:

Promoting Just, Peaceful and Inclusive Societies in Europe and Central Asia

In the Europe and the Commonwealth of Independent States (ECIS) region, UNDP works with local, national and international partners to strengthen inclusive and effective governance and peace, by advocating, advising, enabling discussion, fostering consensus and capacitating institutions. This report provides an overview of UNDP’s governance and peacebuilding work, and serves as a baseline for measuring progress as countries across the region begin working towards the Sustainable Development Goals.
